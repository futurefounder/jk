### 🚀 RemoteNActive - Service
&nbsp;

**What**

A b2b service for remote teams

**How**

*Website*: [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/), [Tailblocks](https://tailblocks.cc/), Javascript Libraries - [Alpine JS](https://github.com/alpinejs/alpine), [Canvas Confetti](https://www.npmjs.com/package/canvas-confetti), [AOS Animation](https://michalsnik.github.io/aos/)

*Service*: Meetings [x.ai](https://x.ai), Playbook [Airtable](https://airtable.com), [Canva](https://canva.com)

**When**

04/2021 prework 05/2021 Start 

**Why**

Remote work is great. But it comes at a cost: social connections.

Team leaders in the modern remote working environment have a lot on their to do list and can spend the time researching remote team building information doing more useful work for their core business. This is why I created a service for remote teams to connect and engage easier by delivering a bespoke remote team playbook.

**Target audience**

Team Leader + HR / Peoples Managers

**Status**

✔️ Live - [Link to GitHub Repository](https://github.com/futurefounder/remotena)

### ✍🏽 iwrite2day
&nbsp;

**What**

A web-based tool to help you develop a writing habit (Personal Project)

**How**

HTML, CSS, Javascript

**When**

03/2021

**Why**

Writing regularly is not easy. I myself noticed the first positive progress after about 8 weeks of daily writing.

I used different templates and websites, but many of them were bloated and took up time.

That's why I created this website with which 99 words can be written. Then you get nice confetti as a reward

**Target Audience**

People who want to build a regular writing habit

**Status**

🧊 On ice - [Link to site](https://www.iwrite2day.xyz)
